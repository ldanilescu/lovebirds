What is LoveBirds

LoveBirds is a a new way to transfer touch and emotions between two people. A couple of 2 smart and beautiful jewels, that can also send you notifications about the things that matter most to you.

LoveBirds connects you and your beloved one as if you never left each other. It is a way to say the most important thing to the most important person. Your beloved is the only one who will feel that you touched your bracelet. No matter how far away you are from each other, it feels like you just touched his hand. Two LoveBirds bracelets are connected only to each other, same as you two are.
